our approach

Empowering sustainable growth in industry

We drive sustainable solutions in factory and industrial settings, focusing on efficiency, innovation, and environmental responsibility to support long-term growth.

We are dedicated to publishing high-quality technical handbooks that provide professionals, youths and entrepreneurs with the expertise they need to flourish in the plastics industry. Through education, mentorship and hands-on skill development, we attempt to bridge gap between theoretical learning and practical application, turning knowledge into action. Our mission is to inspire progress, drive innovations, uplift communities and contribute to a responsible and thriving future for plastics

We strive to be the leading platform for technical knowledge and innovation in the plastics industry - connecting people, sharing expertise, and inspiring new ideas. Through information sharing, education and technical excellence, we wish to empower individuals with insights to create, grow, and contribute to a sustainable and thriving / blooming / flourishing (to keep any one word) plastics industry

At Dave Informdia, we believe that knowledge fuels innovation and drives success. Through our plastic technical handbooks, we empower students, entrepreneurs, and industry professionals with valuable insights, helping them shape the future of plastics. More than just publishing—we’re building a platform for learning, entrepreneurship, and industry progress.

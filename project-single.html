Global Entrepreneurship

In Today’s world we often hear these words:

  • Economy Growth
  • Employment Generation
  • Poverty Eradication
  • Child Education
  • Food & Nutrition
  • Women Empowerment

And others…. Is there one thing that would change all of above and bring about a Positive change?

What is the SOLUTION?

It’s Money!!! YES, a sustainable income in hands of all is the Solution to all above.

How?

We at Dave Technical Service believe that:

  • Offering Business Opportunity in hands of common people
  • Creating conducive environment and offering a platform to enable Micro Industry development from the grass root level in the country
  • Encouraging SMEs and New Start-ups
  • Low Cost (Ranging from 4 – 9 Hundred Thousand INR)
  • Single-phase operating (Work in Home / Office / Garage / Farm – No industrial zone required – Non Polluting)
  • Fully Auto with PLC controls yet has Simple operations
  • Single machine caters to the requirement of different industries providing wide flexibility
  • Each project will generate Employment for 2 – 4 people
  • Developed a Policy which if implemented will ensure revenue generation for these start-ups

Once consistent income (source of living) is generated and assured, issues of Child Education, Food & Nutrition, Welfare, Poverty, Women Empowerment and others will start getting addressed.

We look forward to work with Local Governments, NGOs, Industry chambers, Associations, and others for their support and cooperation in our efforts to provide small projects and earning opportunities with employment generation in the country.

This would bring about positive change in the lives of people and aid to the economic development of the regions and country at large.

Goals of the project

The primary goals of the Total Quality Management (TQM) project are to enhance product quality, reduce defects, and foster a culture of continuous improvement.

  • Enhance product quality and consistency
  • Increase employee involvement and ownership
  • Foster a culture of continuous improvement
  • Improve supplier quality management
